Action gate (Network Provisioning Governor)

HARD holds cannot be overridden by a human approver. Right-of-way/carrier-license spec-basis, evidence completeness, live route endpoints and route-capacity are independently recomputed, never trusted from the advisor's proposal; activating or releasing a real optical circuit is always a human network operator's call, at every rollout phase.

OpGate
:demand/intakephase-3 auto-commit when clean, no capital risk yet -- the ONLY auto-eligible op in this domain
:license/verifyALWAYS human approval · spec-basis independently checked against netops.facts, never fabricated
:route/screenALWAYS human approval when clean · a capacity-blocked route (no free DWDM wavelength) is a HARD, un-overridable hold instead
:actuation/provision-lightpathALWAYS human approval · activates a real optical circuit · route endpoints independently recomputed against the live topology + double-provisioning guard enforced, never auto at any phase
:actuation/teardown-lightpathALWAYS human approval · releases a real optical circuit · double-teardown guard enforced, never auto at any phase
